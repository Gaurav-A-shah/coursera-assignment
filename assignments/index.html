Lilium is a genus of herbaceous flowering palnts growing from blubs, all with large prominent flowers. Lilies are a group of flowering plants which are important in culture and literature in much of the world.

Nelumbo nucifera,also known as Indian lotus,scared lotus,bean of Indian,Egyptian bean or simply lotus,is one of the two extant species of aquatic plant in the family Nelumbonaceae.It is often colloquailly called a water lily.
